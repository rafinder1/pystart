## Conditional instructions

#### <li> Equals: a `==` b </li>
#### <li> Not Equals: a `!=` b </li>
#### <li> Less than: a `<` b </li>
#### <li> Less than or equal to: a `<=` b </li>
#### <li> Greater than: a `>` b </li>
#### <li> Greater than or equal to: a `>=` b </li>

## Conjunction and Disjunction
| a   | b   | Conjunction (and) | Disjunction (or) |
|-----|-----|-------------------|------------------|
| YES | YES | YES               | YES              |
| NO  | YES | NO                | YES              |
| YES | NO  | NO                | YES              |
| NO  | NO  | NO                | NO               |
